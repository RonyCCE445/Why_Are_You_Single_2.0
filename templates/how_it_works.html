How It Works (Scientifically-ish)

"You talk. We analyze. Then gently roast you with science."
Here’s an overview of the techy magic behind your existential crisis.

The Two Paths

You get two equally judgmental options: Write an Intro or Take the Quiz.

- The Intro reflects how you see yourself.
- The Quiz reveals how the app sees you — based on your choices, logic, and emotional damage.

Intro Path: NLP & Machine Learning

When you write your intro, it gets analyzed using a Natural Language Processing (NLP) pipeline powered by a trained Machine Learning model.

Here's what happens:

  • → Your text is cleaned, tokenized, and vectorized using a TF-IDF approach.
  • → A classification model (like Naive Bayes or similar) predicts your MBTI type based on patterns learned from hundreds of labeled samples.
  • → Basically, we judge your soul by your sentence structure. Cute, right?

Quiz Path: Trait-Based Inference

The quiz isn’t just random nonsense — every question is designed to reflect one of the MBTI dichotomies: E/I, S/N, T/F, J/P.

Here's how we decode you:

  • → Each answer you choose is tagged with a corresponding trait.
  • → We tally your responses and infer the dominant letters based on your behavioral leanings.
  • → Your final MBTI is a reflection of how you behave when you're being guided — a.k.a., when the app steers the ship.

Interpretation Layer

After we detect your MBTI, the app cross-references it with a few lovingly handpicked datasets:

  • ✔ Personality traits unique to your type
  • ✔ Rarity of your type in the general population (or internet dwellers)
  • ✔ Famous personalities that share your code (allegedly)
You also get a sarcastic roast — because honesty hurts but jokes heal.

Behind every judgment lies code, caffeine, and a questionable sense of humor.
